This game is a well known word game, especialy for Kosovans who watch Oxygen Show.

The rules of the game are simple.

As you can tell, the game board consists of four columns, each of which have four words and final solution at the bottom. The column solution is a word that connects in some way or another with each of the words above it.

The final word is derived from the four column solutions and follows the same rule: It is connected somehow with those four column solutions.

This was a very fun project to work on and gave me a lot of challenges that helped me get better at DOM manipulation.

Although, for the moment, has only one level, I definitely plan on adding a lot more since it is fun for me and I see it being used by groups of friend who want to have a good time playing some word games.
