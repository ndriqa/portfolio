This is an app I made to store a personal color palette to use your favorite colors later in your projects.

I made this app using Kotlin and used the Firebase Authentication system and Realtime Database for user authentications and storing the colors.

I hope it is at least somewhat useful to you. If you want to download this app, I have published it on the Play Store, so just follow the link at the bottom of the page.

This was a fun personal project. I will keep updating it if I find any major bugs, otherwise, I have other stuff I prioritise. Thank you for tuning in. Have a blast!
