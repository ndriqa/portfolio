This is more of a collection of apps rather than a simple project

The whole goal behind this project is to help elementary school kids vizualise their lessons so they can understand them easier.

I try to make the apps as kid-friendly as possible and I hope they have fun while learning and playing at the same time.

I know that just 3 apps are not enough, which is why I'm definitely going to work on some more.

All of these apps are made in plain JavaScript, without any other framework
